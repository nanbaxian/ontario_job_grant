FlashCoding.ca Website Update Brief

Purpose
- Add a clear corporate training entry point to the current FlashCoding website.
- Keep the existing main positioning intact: AI that is genuinely useful for business.
- Support Ontario Job Grant related employer-facing training messaging without making the homepage look like a government subsidy page.

Business Context
- FlashCoding is a Canadian SMB-focused website and systems company.
- The new training offering should feel like a natural extension of the current business, not a separate company.
- The website should communicate practical workforce AI training for business operations, marketing, customer communication, inventory, and content workflows.

Primary Goal
- Introduce a dedicated training section that helps employers understand what FlashCoding teaches, who it is for, and how the training is delivered.

Recommended Scope
1. Add one new top-level navigation item
2. Add one new homepage section
3. Add one new standalone page

Recommended Navigation
- Current: HOME | SOLUTIONS | PROJECTS | BLOG | PROFILE | CONTACT
- Update to: HOME | SOLUTIONS | TRAINING | PROJECTS | BLOG | PROFILE | CONTACT

Language guidance
- Use `TRAINING` in English.
- Use `企业培训` in Chinese.
- Avoid `COURSES` if the intent is employer-facing workforce upskilling.

Homepage Change
- Add one new section on the homepage.
- Suggested placement: after `Choose The Direction That Fits` and before `Why Choose Us`.
- This section should be a secondary entry point, not the main hero.

Homepage Section Positioning
- Title: AI Workforce Training
- Subtitle: Practical AI training for teams that need to improve daily operations, customer work, marketing output, and internal productivity.

Homepage Section Content
- AI Operations Training
  - Order management, customer workflows, inventory processes, and internal reporting.
- AI Marketing & Content Training
  - AI-assisted campaign planning, content generation, social media workflow, and publishing automation.
- AI Automation Readiness
  - Help teams understand where AI fits, what should be automated, and how to use AI responsibly.

Homepage CTA
- Button text: View Corporate Training
- Alternative text: Explore Training Programs
- CTA should link to the new training page.

New Page
- Add one dedicated page: `/training`
- Keep the page focused and practical.
- Do not create too many new pages at the start.

Training Page Structure
1. Overview
2. Who This Is For
3. Training Programs
4. Delivery Formats
5. Employer Documentation Available
6. Contact Form

Suggested Training Programs
1. AI Order Management
2. AI Marketing Management
3. AI Customer Management
4. AI Inventory Management
5. AI Content Generation & Automated Publishing

Recommended Positioning
- Use: Corporate AI Workforce Training
- Use: Practical AI training for business teams
- Use: Workforce upskilling
- Avoid overly grant-driven language on the public page

Suggested Public Copy Direction
- FlashCoding provides practical AI workforce training for Canadian small and mid-sized businesses.
- The training helps teams improve productivity, reduce repetitive work, and build responsible AI usage habits.
- Training can include course outlines, quotations, attendance records, and completion evidence for employer records.

What Not To Do
- Do not make Ontario Job Grant the main headline of the homepage.
- Do not replace the existing corporate website positioning.
- Do not turn the site into a government-program landing page.
- Do not expand into many new pages before the first training page is live.

SEO Guidance
- Keep the public page clear and easy to quote.
- Use strong headings and short explanatory sections.
- Include FAQ content if possible.
- Make sure the page title and description clearly mention corporate training and AI workforce training.
- Keep the content aligned with the current business identity and service history.

Suggested SEO Titles
- FlashCoding | Corporate AI Workforce Training
- Practical AI Workforce Training for Canadian Businesses
- AI Training for Business Teams | FlashCoding

Suggested SEO Description
- FlashCoding provides practical AI workforce training for Canadian businesses, including operations, marketing, customer workflows, inventory, and content automation.

Acceptance Criteria
- The homepage shows a new training entry point.
- The top navigation includes `TRAINING`.
- The new training page exists at `/training`.
- The page explains what FlashCoding teaches in practical business terms.
- The page supports employer-facing training conversations.
- The page does not dilute the current main website identity.

Implementation Priority
1. Navigation update
2. Homepage training section
3. Training page creation
4. SEO metadata
5. Contact form wiring if needed

Notes
- This brief is intended for website execution, not for grant application language.
- Keep the tone business-first, practical, and employer-friendly.
- The training section should read like a real service offering, not a funding pitch.
